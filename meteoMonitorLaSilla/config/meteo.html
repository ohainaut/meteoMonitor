FORECAST

ESO

Based on weather forecast by ESO's Astroclimatology team, lead by Marc Sarazin (ESO's weather and observing condition studies specialist), on whose page one can find lots of information.
  • Forecast(0-30h) for La Silla, Paranal and Chajnantor.
  • Long range outlook (2-8 days) for La Silla, Paranal and Chajnantor.
  • Weather watch: summary
  • Pressure, Temperature and Wind Forecasts: Numeric values and evolution plots
  • Precipitable Water Vapour and Cirrus Cloud Cover: Launch the         Java forecast applet          (from ESO weather forecast's Water Vapor and Cirrus Page).

PAST INFO

  • ESO Ambiant Conditions Database: This graphic interface lets you query the weather database for La Silla and Paranal since Aug.1998
  • LOSSAM: Line of Sight Sky Absorption Monitor, i.e. "was the night photometric". Updated daily.
    • La Silla LOSSAM for last night,
    • Paranal LOSSAM for last night,
    • LSO and PO LOSSAM Archive.
  • SCIDAR: Scintillometer experiment at La Silla.

Ephemerides

  • The Daily Almanac (by Brian Casey , using Skycalc by by John Thorstensen) gives all the info for a given night.
  • For specific objects: Object Seasonal Observability, and the Hourly Airmass Table (by Brian Casey, using Skycalc by by John Thorstensen)
  • Airmass plots for your objects
